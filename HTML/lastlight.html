During the second semester of studying at USM, we've been learning basics of the Unity 2D and I tried to create a simple 2D Platformer called "Last Light".

The bright soul guy whose name is Art, is trying to find an escape from the complete and deep darkness.

In order to do that, he needs to find a missing element from the portal, which turn on the lights and transgress him back to the bright world.

During the journey, some enemies will try to stop and kill our hero, so he should avoid or eliminate them.


Inspired by LIMBO and Metro 2033 games.
